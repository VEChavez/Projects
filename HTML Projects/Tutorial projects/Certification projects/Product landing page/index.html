FAQ

Have questions about our pet sitting services? Our FAQ section provides answers to common inquiries, helping you understand what we offer and how we can meet your pet’s needs. If you have any other questions, feel free to reach out to us directly.

  • What services do you offer?

    We offer daily pet sitting visits, overnight pet care, and pet taxi services. Each service is designed to ensure your pet receives the best care while you’re away.

  • How do I book a service?

    You can book our services by contacting us through our website’s booking form or by giving us a call. We'll discuss your needs and schedule a convenient time for you and your pet.

  • What is included in the daily pet sitting visits?

    Daily pet sitting visits include feeding, providing fresh water, playtime, basic grooming, and medication administration if needed. We also tidy up and clean litter boxes as part of our service.

  • How does the overnight pet care work?

    Our overnight pet care service includes a pet sitter staying at your home from evening until morning. This service includes evening and morning feeding, walks, playtime, and overnight supervision to ensure your pet is well cared for throughout the night.

  • What is the pet taxi service?

    The pet taxi service provides safe and reliable transportation for your pets to appointments, grooming sessions, or special events. We use a comfortable vehicle equipped with pet restraints to ensure your pet's safety and comfort during the trip.

  • Can I meet the pet sitter before booking?

    Yes, we encourage you to meet with your assigned pet sitter before booking to ensure you’re comfortable with them and to discuss any specific needs or preferences for your pet.

  • How do you ensure the safety of my pet?

    We prioritize the safety of your pet by using secure and comfortable equipment, following detailed care instructions, and regularly training our staff. We also provide regular updates and are always available to address any concerns.

  • What happens if my pet needs medical attention?

    If your pet requires medical attention, we will follow your instructions and take them to the vet or contact you immediately if any issues arise. We are trained to handle basic medical needs and will ensure your pet gets the care they need.
